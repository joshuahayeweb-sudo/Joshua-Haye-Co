Privacy Policy & Terms of Service

1. Introduction

Welcome to Joshua Haye & Co. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data.

2. Information We Collect

We collect and process the following types of information:

  • Personal identification information (name, email address, phone number, TRN)
  • Business information (company name, registration numbers, financial records)
  • Financial information necessary for tax and audit services
  • Communication records between you and our firm

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used for:

  • Providing professional accounting and auditing services
  • Tax preparation and filing with relevant authorities
  • Regulatory compliance and reporting requirements
  • Communicating important updates about our services
  • Improving our service quality and client experience

4. Professional Standards

We maintain the highest professional standards in accordance with:

  • Institute of Chartered Accountants of Jamaica (ICAJ) regulations
  • International Standards on Auditing (ISA)
  • Professional Ethics Standards
  • Anti-Money Laundering (AML) requirements

5. Data Protection & Security

We protect your data through:

  • Industry-standard encryption for all digital communications
  • Secure physical storage for paper documents
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Strict access controls and authentication procedures
  • Employee confidentiality agreements

6. Client Confidentiality

We maintain strict client confidentiality by:

  • Never sharing client information without explicit consent
  • Implementing need-to-know access policies
  • Secure disposal of outdated documents
  • Regular staff training on confidentiality procedures

7. Your Rights

As our client, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal information in our records
  • Request corrections to inaccurate information
  • Receive copies of your financial records
  • Be informed about how your data is being used
  • Request deletion of your information (subject to regulatory requirements)

8. Regulatory Compliance

We comply with:

  • Companies Act of Jamaica
  • Tax Administration Jamaica (TAJ) requirements
  • Financial Services Commission (FSC) regulations
  • International Financial Reporting Standards (IFRS)
